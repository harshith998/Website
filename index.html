What is Elysian Tree?

Elysian Tree is a Modern and Historical Art museum with works from all over the world established by Siddhartha Milkuri and designed by Charan Chitturi. In its 2304 acres of area, there are nature trails, venues, exhibitions, art studios, and a dining hall.

CALENDAR

The Elysian Tree Museum constantly cycles through its pieces, and always has a new exhibition to explore. All of our temporary/seasonal events and exhibition dates are on our calender. Our year long exhibits down below showcase all forms of art throughout history.

ABSTRACT

Elysian Tree Museum of World Art has many abstract art exhibitions, each having an unique story to tell,

ENGINEERING

Apart from the building itself, many exhibitions, from mixed media to installations, show us engineering in the form of art.

CONTEMPERARY

The War To End All Wars: Our brand new gallary showcases art made during World War 2.

CULTURE

Twisted Fruit Blossom: In this historical gallary, we have curated hundreds of pieces throughout Asia's history.

Normal Hours

Our standard museum hours are 11 a.m. - 6 p.m. on Mondays and Wednesdays, 11 a.m. - 8 p.m. on Thursdays and Fridays, and 10 a.m. - 6 p.m. on Saturdays and Sundays.
Note: These timings may change based on venue bookings and Holidays.

Tours

To sign up for our tours (which are available daily), sign up at the front desk 15 minutes prior to the tour. To get more information, call our number or email us!

Amenities

Elysian Tree Museum of World Art is
more than just an Art Museum.

Restaurant and Coffee Bar

The Resteraunt and Coffee Bar offers dining for guests on a glass-enclosed bridge overlooking the ponds.

Museum Store

Designed by architect Marlon Blackwell, the store allows you to purchase a memoir of your Elysian Tree experience.

Library

The library features more than 50,000 volumes of art reference material.

Trails and Sculptures

10 miles of walking trails link the museum's 600-acre park and wildlife enclosure to the museum itself and other facilities.

Venues

Elysian Tree Museum of World Art has many locations that are a perfect to host a party/event at. Contact us for more information.
