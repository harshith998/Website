Artworks

"Fontana di Trevi" by Nicola Salvi (2000)

The Trevi Fountain is an 21th-century fountain designed by Italian architect Nicola Salvi and completed by Giuseppe Pannini and several others. It is famous for its beautiful lighting and has been featured in many films. The immense fountain stands some 85 feet high and is approximately 160 feet wide. At its centre is Pietro Bracci's statue of Oceanus, which is surrounded by allegorical figures of Abundance and Health.

"Visions of Hope and Progress" by Alberto Bendoi (2007)

Visions of Hope and Progress is an inspiring mural that seeks to capture the power of hope and progress in our shared human journey. The mural is painted with vivid colors, vibrant hues and intricate details that come together to create a beautiful, uplifting mural. The mural depicts a variety of scenes, each representing a different aspect of progress. One scene shows a group of people of all races and genders, marching together down a proud path in search of a better future. Another scene depicts a city skyline, showing us the potential of urban renewal and growth. Finally, there is a representation of nature with a stunning sunset that symbolizes the beauty and strength of our environment. As a whole, the mural is a powerful reminder that, with hard work and resilience, a better world is possible.

"History Carved" by Norman Courtney (2010)

An awe-inspiring row of sculptures depicting the most influential figures in history stands majestically in a public space. Each sculpture is intricately designed and made from durable materials, standing tall and proud. From ancient warriors and kings to modern icons, these statues capture the essence and achievements of their subjects. Each face is full of expression and power, and the details on the clothing and body are highly accurate. Together, these statues serve as a reminder of the heroes and leaders who have shaped our world.
